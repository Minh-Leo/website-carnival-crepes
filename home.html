About Us

What do you expect of the food when you enter a carnival? Perhaps a tasty, fuss-free bite with a bit of loose change? Carnival Crepes, Shakes & Caffeine was inspired by this concept.

Carnival is all about an experience, so whether sweet or savoury, our menu is thought with love to ensure that it brings excitement to our customers tastebuds and a smile to their faces.

Whether an early riser or late-night owl, you can count on us for a convenient tuck-in.

Our goal is to create a brand that allows crepes and shakes to shine in the Melbourne foodie scene by continuing to innovate different flavour profiles with exciting combinations to ensure that crowds return time to time to enjoy the vibe of the Carnival.

Our promise to you:

Everyone is welcomed at Carnival! We hope to bring excitement through our food and service and smiles to everyone who walks through our doors.

With a bit of loose change, you can count on us to serve you a tasty bite!

Whether sweet or savoury, our creative flavour profiles will be sure to tantalise your tastebuds wanting you to return back for more!
